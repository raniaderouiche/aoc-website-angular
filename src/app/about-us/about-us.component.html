About Us

Art of Code Club is a collaboration between IT Students of the Faculty of Science of Bizerte in order to learn and excel in Computer Science field.

Our goals:

Education

Providing essential training and workshops to equip all students with fundamental programming and development knowledge.

Collaboration

Fostering collaboration among students across all departments with the aim of learning and excelling in the IT field.

Innovation

Striving to take on new challenges and push boundaries, we continue to innovate, grow, and inspire our members to reach their full potential in the ever-evolving world of technology.
