History

A brief look into our story.

  • December 2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Within the Faculty of Sciences of Bizerte, a group of friends and colleagues banded together to establish this club with a visionary goal: to cultivate a community where we can can learn, grow and innovate.

  • 2022-2023

    Growing the Community

    Through our wide range of events and activities we were able to attract members and foster an environment for collaboration and knowledge-sharing.

  • 2023-2024

    Innovation in Action: Milestones in Projects and Events

    Leveling up our competitions, workshops, and partnerships propelled the club to new heights, cementing its reputation as a hub for student excellence.

  • 2024-2025

    Expansion: Reaching New Heights

    Preparing for the next steps by taking in on our biggest project yet in collaboration with Tunisian American Young Professionals (TAYP).
